1.Brownie

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 2 days
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
butter 150 gr
sugar 100 gr
flour 90 gr
dark chocolate 90 gr

Step 1: Mix eggs,melted butter,melted chocolate.
Step 2: Mix all dry ingredients.
Step 3: Set oven on 180 degrees and cook for 25 minutes.

2.Cheesecake

  • Time: 4 hours
  • Serves: 3 days
Ingredients Quantity
eggs 2
special cheese 300 gr
cream 100 ml
dough 200 gr

Step 1:: cook the cream.
Step 2: add to dough.
